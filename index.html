DPChallenge Award Gallery

DPChallenge is a website with a user-centred community of photographers. It thas been in existence since 2002 and has hosted over 3,000 challenges. The members of DPChallenge participate first by submitting images, and after by voting on these images, and the images that receive the highest average vote are awarded with virtual ribbons on the website. These ribbons are also displayed in the user's profile.
